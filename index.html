About Me

Climate scientist and data science expert with 5+ years developing AI-driven solutions

I am a climate scientist and data science expert with a PhD in Interdisciplinary Engineering from Northeastern University. My research focuses on extreme weather modeling and developing AI-driven solutions for precipitation forecasting and flood risk assessment.

Currently serving as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Institute of Experiential AI, I have led multi-institutional projects involving NASA, national laboratories, and federal agencies. My work translates academic research into practical risk management tools for stakeholders including Fortune-level organizations.

With expertise spanning machine learning, remote sensing, climate modeling, and hydrological engineering, I am passionate about leveraging cutting-edge technology to address climate challenges and build resilient communities.
